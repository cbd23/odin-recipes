Prep time: 25 mins   |   Cook time: 1h 10min   |   Difficulty: Easy   |   Serves: 6

Prepare this easy vegetarian lasagna using just a handful of ingredients.
You can use ready-made tomato sauce and white sauce, or batch cook the sauces and freeze them.

Ingredients

Main

  • 3 red peppers, cut into large chunks
  • 2 aubergines, cut into ½ cm thick slices
  • 8 tbsp olive oil
  • 300g lasagna sheets
  • 125g mozzarella
  • handful cherry tomatoes, halved

For the tomato sauce

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1 carrot, roughly chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 200ml white wine
  • 3 x 400g cans chopped tomatoes
  • 1 bunch of basil, leaves picked

For the white sauce

  • 85g butter
  • 85g plain flour
  • 750ml milk

Step 1

  1. Heat the olive oil in a saucepan.
  2. Add the onions, garlic and carrot.
  3. Cook for 5-7 mins over a medium heat until softened.
  4. Turn up the heat a little and stir in the tomato purée.
  5. Cook for 1 min, pour in the white wine, then cook for 5 mins until this has reduced by two-thirds.
  6. Pour over the chopped tomatoes and add the basil leaves.
  7. Bring to the boil then simmer for 20 mins.
  8. Leave to cool then whizz in a food processor.

Step 2

  1. To make the white sauce, melt the butter in a saucepan, stir in the plain flour, then cook for 2 mins.
  2. Slowly whisk in the milk, then bring to the boil, stirring.
  3. Turn down the heat, then cook until the sauce starts to thicken and coats the back of a wooden spoon.

Step 3

  1. Heat the oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6.
  2. Lightly oil two large baking trays and add the peppers and aubergines.
  3. Toss with the olive oil, season well, then roast for 25 mins.

Step 4

  1. Reduce the oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4.
  2. Lightly oil a 30 x 20cm ovenproof dish.
  3. Arrange a layer of the vegetables on the bottom, then pour over a third of the tomato sauce.
  4. Top with a layer of lasagna sheets, then drizzle over a quarter of the white sauce.
  5. Repeat until you have three layers of pasta.

Step 5

  1. Spoon the remaining white sauce over the pasta, making sure the whole surface is covered, then scatter over the mozzarella and cherry tomatoes.
  2. Bake for 45 mins until bubbling and golden.
